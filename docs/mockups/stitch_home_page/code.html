Nuestro Desafío 2050

Our overarching goal is to achieve complete carbon neutrality by 2050, transforming our industry through innovation and collective action.

Ejes Estratégicos

layers 35% Clinker, cemento y aditivos
expand_more

Optimizing clinker factor, utilizing supplementary cementitious materials, and innovating with novel additives to lower the carbon footprint of our final products.

local_fire_department 40% Eficiencia energética y combustibles
expand_more

Implementing state-of-the-art technologies to reduce energy consumption and increasing the use of alternative fuels with lower carbon intensity.

bolt 11% Electricidad
expand_more

Transitioning to renewable electricity sources for our operations, including wind, solar, and hydro power, to decarbonize our energy mix.

recycling 9% Carbonatación
expand_more

Leveraging the natural process of carbonation in concrete structures to re-absorb atmospheric CO2 over their lifecycle, creating a carbon sink.

co2 5% Captura de Carbono (CCUS)
expand_more

Investing in and developing Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage technologies to capture remaining emissions at the source and prevent their release into the atmosphere.
